Grundlagen >

NURBS (univariat)

$$ d=1 \; ; \; p_{d} \; ; \; \mathbf{\Xi}_{d}=\left(\xi_{d,1},\dots ,\xi_{d,i},\dots ,\xi_{d,n_{d}+p_{d}+1}\right) \; ; \; \xi_{d}\in\left[\xi_{d,1},\xi_{d,n_{d}+p_{d}+1}\right]_{\mathbb{R}}=\mathit{\Omega}_{d} \; ; \; \\ \mathbf{x}_{i} = \left( x_{i,1} , x_{i,2}, \dots \right)^\intercal \; ; \; w_{i} \; ; \; i\in\left[1,n_{d}\right]_{\mathbb{N}} $$

$$ \mathrm{N}_{d,i,p_{d}}\left(\xi_{d}\right) = \left\{\begin{array}{ll} \left\{\begin{array}{ll} 1 & \text{für } \xi_{d,i}\leq\xi_{d}\lt\xi_{d,i+1} \\ 0 & \text{sonst} \end{array}\right. & \text{für } p_{d}=0 \\ \frac{\displaystyle \xi_{d} - \xi_{d,i}}{\displaystyle \xi_{d,i+p_{d}} - \xi_{d,i}}\,\mathrm{N}_{d,i,p_{d}-1}\left(\xi_{d}\right) + \frac{\displaystyle \xi_{d,i+p_{d}+1} - \xi_{d}}{\displaystyle \xi_{d,i+p_{d}+1} - \xi_{d,i+1}}\,\mathrm{N}_{d,i+1,p_{d}-1}\left(\xi_{d}\right) & \text{für } p_{d} \geq 1 \end{array}\right. $$

$$ \mathbf{X}\left(\xi_{d}\right) = \sum_{i=1}^{n_{d}}{\frac{\mathrm{N}_{d,i,p_{d}}\left(\xi_{d}\right)\; w_{i}}{\sum_{k=1}^{n_{d}}{\mathrm{N}_{d,i,p_{d}}\left(\xi_{d}\right)\; w_{k}}}\; \mathbf{x}_{i}} = \sum_{i=1}^{n_{d}}{\frac{\mathrm{N}_{d,i,p_{d}}\left(\xi_{d}\right)\; w_{i}}{w\left(\xi_{d}\right)}\; \mathbf{x}_{i}} = \sum_{i=1}^{n_{d}}{ \mathrm{R}_{i}\left(\xi_{d}\right) \; \mathbf{x}_{i}} $$

[Piegl, L. und Tiller, W; 1996]

NURBS (multivariat)

$$ p_{d} \; ; \; \mathbf{\Xi}_{d} \; ; \; \underline{\xi}=\left(\xi_{1},\dots,\xi_{d}\right)^\intercal \; ; \; n_{\mathrm{e}}=\prod_{d}{n_{d}} \; ; \; \mathbf{x}_{i} = \left( x_{i,1} , x_{i,2}, \dots \right)^\intercal \; ; \; w_{i} \; ; \; i\in\left[1,n\right]_{\mathbb{N}} $$

$$ \mathrm{N}_{i}\left(\underline{\xi}\right) = \prod_{b=1}^{d}{\mathrm{N}_{b,\tilde{\mathrm{i}}_{\mathrm{inv}}\left(i,\left(n_{d}\right),d,b\right),p_{b}}\left(\xi_{b}\right)} $$

$$ \mathbf{X}\left(\underline{\xi}\right) = \sum_{i=1}^{n}{\frac{\mathrm{N}_{i}\left(\underline{\xi}\right)\; w_{i}}{\sum_{k=1}^{n}{\mathrm{N}_{k}\left(\underline{\xi}\right)\; w_{k}}}\; \mathbf{x}_{i}} = \sum_{i=1}^{n}{\frac{\mathrm{N}_{i}\left(\underline{\xi}\right)\; w_{i}}{w\left(\underline{\xi}\right)}\; \mathbf{x}_{i}} = \sum_{i=1}^{n}{ \mathrm{R}_{i}\left(\underline{\xi}\right) \; \mathbf{x}_{i}} $$

[Piegl, L. und Tiller, W; 1996]

Grundlagen >

IGA Elementformulierung

Transformieren der Bernstein-Basispolynome in die Element-Basisfunktionen:
$$ \mathbf{R}^{\mathrm{L}}_{e} = \tilde{\mathbf{M}}^{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{B}}_{1,e}\;\mathbf{N}^{\mathrm{B}} $$

Assemblieren der Systemmatrizen analog zur FEM:
$$ K_{i,j}^{\mathrm{L}} = \int_{\mathit{\Omega_{1,e}}}{\mathcal{F}}\left[\mathrm{R}_{i}^{L}\left(\xi\right),\mathrm{R}_{j}^{L}\left(\xi\right)\right] \left|\mathbf{J}\left(\xi\right)\right| \mathrm{d}\xi \\ \xi\in\mathit{\Omega}_{1,e} $$

[Borden et al.; 2010]

Grundlagen >

THB

Übersetzung von Level zu Level durch lokale Unterteilungsmatrizen:
$$ \mathbf{R}^{\mathrm{L1}} = \tilde{\mathbf{M}}^{\mathrm{L1},\mathrm{L2}}\;\mathbf{R}^{\mathrm{L2}} \\ \mathbf{x}^{\mathrm{L2}} = \left(\tilde{\mathbf{M}}^{\mathrm{L1},\mathrm{L2}}\right)^\intercal\;\mathbf{x}^{\mathrm{L1}} $$

Herstellen der THB-Levelbeziehungen durch globale red. Unterteilungsmatrix:
$$ \mathbf{K}^{\mathrm{H.mod}} = \tilde{\mathbf{M}}^{\mathrm{H.red}}\;\mathbf{K}^{\mathrm{H}}\;\left(\tilde{\mathbf{M}}^{\mathrm{H.red}}\right)^\intercal \\ \mathbf{f}^{\mathrm{H.mod}} = \tilde{\mathbf{M}}^{\mathrm{H.red}}\;\mathbf{f}^{\mathrm{H}} $$

[Hennig et al.; 2016]
